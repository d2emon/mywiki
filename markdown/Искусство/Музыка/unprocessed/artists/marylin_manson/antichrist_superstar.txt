not tabbing rythym so listen to the song and figure it out.

Now, to try and make sense of all this

play the intro (try and get whoever is listening to you to do the 	"hail" part)
play the second intro
then the verse and sing:
	You built me up with your wishing hell
	I didn't have to sell you
	You threw your money in the pissing well
	You do just what they tell you
play the prechorus: 
	Repent, that's what i'm talking about
	I shed the skin to feed the fake
	Repent, that's what i'm talking about
	Whose mistake am I anyway?
Chorus:
	Cut the head off
	Grows back hard
	I am the Hydra
	Now you'll see your star
solo (just make a bunch of feedback, if you've got a floyd rose, or any
floating tremolo really, you can get it to sound perfect)
Verse:
	Prick your finger it is done
	The moon has now eclipsed the sun
	The angel has spread it's wings
	The time has come for bitter things
Prechorus and chorus
Intro:
	The time has come, it is quite clear
	Our Antichrist is almost here...
					It is done
Chorus (there's another solo in here, but it sounds like it's just a 
	sampled loop of a couple of guitar feeding back, you can mimic it 	by
sticking your guitar's pickups in front of your speaker and 	going crazy
with the toggle switch, it helps if you have a wah-	pedal although it does
change the tone of the feedback)
Intro

there you have it, it's a pretty simple song when you get the hang of it.
