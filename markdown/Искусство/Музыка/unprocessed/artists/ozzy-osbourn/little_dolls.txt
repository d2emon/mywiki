SONG:  Little Dolls
ARTIST: Ozzy Osbourne
WRITTEN BY: Ozzy Osbourne, Randy Rhoads, Bob Daisley, and Lee Kerslake
TRANSCRIBED BY: The HitMan, Krist Smith

I use the idea of different guitars just to provide a way of telling
when to turn on and off the effects.

Guitar 1 - heavily distorted (perfectly, in fact)
Guitar 2 - clean tone
Guitar 3 - heavily distorted and possibly with a flanger or similar effect
Guitar 4 - clean tone with flanger or similar effect
*All guitars are tuned down 1/2 step (1=eb, 2=Bb, 3=Gb, 4=Db, 5=Ab, 6=Eb)


Basically, when you see "guitar 1", just hit the distortion.  It may seem
a lot for nothing, but I want to get as much as possible right.  Randy Rhoads
rules!!!!!!!!
