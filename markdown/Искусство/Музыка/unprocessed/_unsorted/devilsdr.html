"Devil's Dream" is a fairly popular tune for fiddlers in bluegrass and old timey circles. Some Irish bands play it as well.

This tune appeared in 1900 at the latest, and is in the public domain worldwide.

If you like, you can hear a MIDI file of "Devil's Dream".
