"Slides" bookmark field presents all photo information of the disk.

"Page Up", "Page Down" buttons help to sequential viewing of images.

For slide image print right click mouse in the bookmark field.


Bookmark "Slides" control bar:

Bar capabilities:

  • View of images in their record order or in random sequence;
  • "Manual" slides view using "forward"-"backward" buttons or auto view ("start" and "pause" buttons);
  • Set up suspend interval when auto view.


    Bookmark "Video" control bar:

    Bar capabilities:

  • Run, suspend and rewind videos (hook and drag slider to rewind);
  • Stretch and compress video display to bookmark "Video" size.
