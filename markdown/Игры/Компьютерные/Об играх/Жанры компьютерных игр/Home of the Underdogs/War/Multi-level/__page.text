!! Multi-level
		
Games that let you control several levels of the conflict at once, e.g. tactical and strategic (P.T.O. II). Most commonly found in science fiction-themed wargames that feature both and planetside battles (Battles of Destiny)

* L'Empereur
* Gemfire
* Star Legions
* Genghis Khan
* Liberty or Death
* P.T.O. II
* Battles of Destiny
