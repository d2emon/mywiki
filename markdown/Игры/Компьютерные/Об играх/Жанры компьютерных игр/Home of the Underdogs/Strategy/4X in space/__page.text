!! 4X in space
		
Games set in outer space that embody the "eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, eXterminate" paradigm exemplified by SimTex's Master of Orion. Fog of war, research, and city/colony management are integral elements of this subgenre.

* Master of Orion
* Deuteros: The Next Millennium
* Ascendancy
* Emperor of the Fading Suns
* Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri
* Anacreon: Reconstruction 4021
* Alien Legacy
* Fragile Allegiance
