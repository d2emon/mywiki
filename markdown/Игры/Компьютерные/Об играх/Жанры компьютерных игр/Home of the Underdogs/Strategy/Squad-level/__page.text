!! Squad-level
		
Popularized by Mythos' Laser Squad and later X-COM, this subgenre put the player in command of a squad of units. Gameplay is turn-based, with elements such as movement and action points. Squad-level games are also usually mission-based.

* X-COM
* Tactics Ogre: Let Us Cling Together
* Twilight 2000
* Syndicate
* Incubation: Time Is Running Out
