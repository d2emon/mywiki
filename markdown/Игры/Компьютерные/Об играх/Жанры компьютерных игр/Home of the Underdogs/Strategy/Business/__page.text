!! Business

Strategy games that put the player in charge of a company, be it a modern enterprise or a medieval trading empire. Historical trading games such as Machiavelli the Prince and The Patrician make up a significant portion of this subgenre.

* Machiavelli The Prince
* Buzz Aldrin's Race into Space
* Railroad Tycoon
* Monopoly Tycoon
* 1830: Railroads & Robber Barons
* Crime Fighter
